Exercise 01
Dead Bug
Deep core Lower belly tone

Trains your deepest core muscles to stabilize your spine — helping you look and feel more pulled-in through your waist.

How to do it

1

Lie on your back, knees bent at 90°, feet lifted. Arms straight up to the ceiling.

2

Press your lower back into the floor. Take a slow breath in.

3

Exhale: slowly lower your right arm overhead AND left leg straight — both together, hovering just above the floor.

4

Breathe in, return to start. Switch sides. That's one rep.

6–8 reps per side · 2–3 sets · Rest 30 sec

❆ Pro Tips

  • • Keep your lower back pressed flat the whole time — don't let it arch.
  • • Slower = harder. Take 3–4 seconds to lower each limb.

Exercise 02
Bird-Dog
Core + balance Back relief

Builds balance, stability, and a stronger back — perfect for reducing that desk-work ache.

How to do it

1

Start on all fours — hands under shoulders, knees under hips. Back flat like a table.

2

Pull your belly button gently in.

3

Extend right arm forward + left leg back simultaneously. Hold 2–3 seconds.

4

Return without rocking. Switch sides. That's one rep.

8 reps per side · 2–3 sets · Rest 30 sec

❆ Pro Tips

  • • Don't let your hips twist or tilt — keep them perfectly level.
  • • Imagine balancing a cup of water on your lower back.

Exercise 03
Forearm Plank
Full core Posture power

The classic that works everything at once — abs, back, shoulders, and legs. One strong, connected line.

How to do it

1

Forearms on the floor, elbows under shoulders. Clasp hands or keep them flat.

2

Step feet back and lift hips — straight line from head to heels.

3

Squeeze core, glutes, and thighs. Breathe normally. Hold.

4

Lower down gently. Rest. Repeat.

20–40 sec hold · 3 sets · Rest 45 sec

❆ Pro Tips

  • • Don't let hips sag or pike up — find that flat, strong line.
  • • Drop to your knees if needed — quality over duration, always.

Exercise 04
Stomach Vacuum
Deepest ab muscle Waist cinch

The secret weapon. Activates your transverse abdominis — the deepest ab muscle that acts like a natural corset.

How to do it

1

Stand, sit, or lie on your back — all positions work.

2

Take a normal breath in, then exhale ALL air out completely.

3

Without breathing in, pull your belly button in and UP toward your spine.

4

Hold 10–15 seconds, then breathe normally. Repeat.

5–8 holds of 10–15 sec · 2 sets · Rest 30 sec

❆ Pro Tips

  • • Best on an empty stomach in the morning — you will feel it so much more clearly.
  • • Can be done at your desk or on the BTS. No one will ever know!

Exercise 05
Glute Bridge
Glutes + lower core Hip strength

Builds strong glutes and lower core, relieves back tension, and adds lovely shape. One of the most rewarding moves you can do.

How to do it

1

Lie on your back, knees bent, feet flat hip-width apart. Arms relaxed at sides.

2

Press through your heels and squeeze your glutes as you lift your hips.

3

Hold at the top 2 seconds — really squeeze!

4

Lower slowly. Don't let hips crash — control it.

12–15 reps · 3 sets · Rest 30 sec

❆ Pro Tips

  • • Focus on that squeeze at the top — that's where the magic happens.
  • • Feet close enough that you can almost touch your heels with your fingertips.
